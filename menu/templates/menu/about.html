About Us

Welcome to Tavern, where every bite counts. Nestled in the heart of Kochi, Tavern is more than just a restaurant; it is a celebration of culinary artistry and the joy of sharing good food. Our journey began with a vision to create a dining experience that marries tradition with innovation, bringing together the rich flavors of local cuisine with the finest international techniques. Each dish on our menu is a testament to this vision, crafted with passion, precision, and a deep respect for the ingredients we use.

At Tavern, we believe in the power of food to tell stories and create lasting memories. Our chefs are storytellers, using the freshest produce and finest ingredients to create dishes that are as visually stunning as they are delicious. From hearty pub classics that evoke the comfort of home to artisanal creations that push the boundaries of culinary artistry, our menu is designed to delight, surprise, and satisfy.

Our flagship location in Kochi serves as the heart of our operations, a place where tradition meets contemporary elegance. Here, in the vibrant setting of Fort Kochi, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the essence of quality and taste. But our story doesn’t end in Kochi. Each Tavern outlet across India is uniquely tailored to reflect the distinctive character and culinary heritage of its location, ensuring that every visit offers a new and exciting experience.

In Bangalore, Tavern Urban captures the dynamic spirit of the city with a menu that combines local favorites with innovative dishes inspired by the cosmopolitan vibe of Indiranagar. Tavern Downtown in Mumbai offers a sophisticated blend of flavors that mirror the eclectic and fast-paced lifestyle of Colaba. Our Delhi location, Tavern Westside, embraces the rich culinary traditions of Connaught Place, serving dishes that celebrate both history and modernity.

In Hyderabad, Tavern Heights brings the vibrant tastes of Jubilee Hills to life with a menu that highlights the city's famous spices and flavors. Tavern Central in Kolkata pays homage to the city’s legendary gastronomic culture with a menu that features both beloved classics and contemporary twists. Finally, Tavern Riverside in Pune offers a serene dining experience in Koregaon Park, where fresh, local ingredients are the stars of every dish.

We understand that dining is more than just a meal; it is an experience. Whether you are enjoying a casual lunch, a festive dinner, or a special celebration, we strive to make every visit to Tavern memorable. Our attentive staff, cozy ambiance, and thoughtfully curated menu ensure that you feel at home from the moment you walk through our doors.

Join us at Tavern, where every bite counts, and every meal is a journey. Discover the flavor, savor the experience, and become part of the Tavern family. We look forward to welcoming you to our table.
